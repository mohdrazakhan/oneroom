Frequently Asked Questions

Is OneRoom free?

Yes! The basic version of OneRoom is free to use for unlimited roommates.

How do I invite roommates?

You can invite roommates via a shared link or QR code directly from the Room settings menu.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption to protect your personal and financial data.
